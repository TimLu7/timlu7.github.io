Grand Prismatic Spring

The Grand Prismatic Spring in Yellowstone National Park is the largest hot spring in the United States, and the third largest in the world.

Soda Butte Creek

Soda Butte Creek is an approximately 20 miles long major tributary of the Lamar River in Yellowstone National Park. It is named for a now-extinct geyser near its mouth.
